# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Women using GLP-1 medication who are concerned by increased visible shedding during rapid weight loss and want a calm, practical hair-wellness routine.

## Product Purpose

Explain why rapid weight loss can affect the hair cycle, help visitors evaluate a nutritional support option, and let them purchase the existing Bloomli gummies product without creating a duplicate catalog product.

## Brand Personality

Warm, grounded, and quietly confident. Bloomli sounds like a smart friend with a science background, never like a clinic or a hype-driven supplement ad.

## Anti-references

Generic card grids, overstuffed promotional banners, clinical fear tactics, miracle language, gradient text, glassmorphism, hero metric templates, colored side stripes, and copied competitor claims.

## Design Principles

1. Explain before selling.
2. Make the product purchase experience feel native to Bloomli and operationally truthful.
3. Use imagery and whitespace to create pace, not decoration for its own sake.
4. Keep claims measured, specific, and easy to verify.
5. Preserve one source of product truth across landing and product pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Keep visible focus states, semantic headings, native disclosure controls, reduced-motion support, readable line lengths, and sufficient contrast.
